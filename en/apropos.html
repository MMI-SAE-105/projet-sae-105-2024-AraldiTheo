About me

My name is Théo Araldi, I am a first-year student at the IUT of Montbéliard in MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions). This site was created as part of an individual project on the theme of the prehistory of the future, where I chose to work on money. Indeed, there has always been an evolution in the means of payment, and this will continue. I wonder what kind of method we will use in the more or less near future.
